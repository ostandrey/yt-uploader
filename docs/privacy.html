Privacy Policy

Coin Wire · Last updated: July 4, 2026

Coin Wire is an automated English-language crypto news publisher (Telegram, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Threads). This policy describes how we handle information related to our publishing tools and channels.

Information we process

Our automation systems process publicly available news headlines and summaries from third-party RSS sources, generate short-form video and text posts, and publish them to social platforms using official APIs.

Account credentials

OAuth tokens and API keys for social platforms are stored securely on our private servers and are used only to publish Coin Wire content. We do not sell personal data.

Audience data

Analytics shown inside TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, or Telegram remain under those platforms’ policies. We do not operate a separate consumer app that collects end-user profiles.
